Resume Highlights

Work

For the past two semesters, I have worked as a Teaching Assistant for the Duke Department of Computer Science, first for Intro to Computer Science, an fundamentals based course taught in python, and then for Data Structures and Algorithms, taught in Java. I also work as a receptionist in Duke's Office of the Vice President.

Play

In my spare time, I am on the Fall Preparation Team of Duke Catalyst, a pre-professional tech organization, a student mentor for the Duke chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery, a section leader and social chair of the Duke University Wind Symphony, and a member of the Duke University Saxophone Quartet.

Activities

I currently volunteer at Mobius Science Center and Spark Community Center in Spokane, Washington. There, I help with workshops and events related to teaching kids robotics and coding using technology such as Little Bits, Ozobots, Scratch, and Code.org.
